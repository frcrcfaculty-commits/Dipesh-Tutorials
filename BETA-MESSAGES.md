## How to Use These Templates

1. Copy the message template that matches the recipient's role
2. Replace all placeholders in `[BRACKETS]`:
   - `[Name]` — recipient ka naam
   - `[APP_URL]` — app ka URL (Hansal se lein)
   - `[YOUR_EMAIL]` — unka login email
   - `[YOUR_PASSWORD]` — unka password
   - `[PASSWORD]` — Dipesh Sir ka password
3. Paste into WhatsApp and send!

> ⚠️ **Important:** Passwords dene ke baad recipients ko recommend karein ki woh apna password badlein — `Settings > Account > Change Password` se.
