## How to Add a New Student

1. From the Dashboard, click **"Students"** in the left sidebar
2. Click the **"+ Add Student"** button (top right)
3. Fill in: Name, Standard, Roll Number, Gender, Parent Name, Phone, Email
4. Click **"Add Student"** at the bottom
5. The student will appear in the list

[SCREENSHOT: Add Student Form]

## How to Mark Today's Attendance

1. Click **"Attendance"** in the left sidebar
2. Select today's date from the calendar
3. Find each student's row in the list
4. Click: **Present (green)** | **Late (yellow)** | **Absent (red)**
5. Saved automatically — no save button needed

[SCREENSHOT: Attendance Page]

---

## How to Enter Test Marks

1. Click **"Test Results"** in the left sidebar
2. Click **"+ Record Marks"**
3. Select the **Test Name**, **Standard**, and **Subject**
4. For each student, enter their **marks obtained**
5. Click **"Save Marks"**

[SCREENSHOT: Enter Test Marks]

---

## How to Check Fee Status and Record a Payment

1. Click **"Billing"** in the left sidebar
2. You will see each student's status: **Paid / Pending / Overdue**
3. Find the student who paid — click **"+ Record Payment"**
4. Enter the **amount paid** and **payment date**
5. The system updates the status automatically

[SCREENSHOT: Fee Status Page]

---

## How to Send a Notification to All Parents

1. Click **"Notifications"** in the left sidebar
2. Click **"+ New Notification"**
3. Write your **title** (e.g., "Fee Reminder")
4. Write the **message**, then click **"Send Notification"**

[SCREENSHOT: Send Notification Page]

## FAQ

**Q: Can parents see each other's data?**
No. Each parent sees only their own child.

**Q: Can I download a student list?**
Yes. Go to Students, click **"Export"**.

**Q: Can I print fee receipts?**
Yes. Go to Billing, click **"Print Report"**.
