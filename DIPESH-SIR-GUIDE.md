# Dipesh Tutorials App — Quick Start Guide

Welcome to the Dipesh Tutorials app! This guide will help you get started in just a few minutes.

---

## How to Login

1. Open the app on your phone or computer
2. Enter your registered email address
3. Enter your password
4. Tap **Sign In**

> **Tip:** If you forget your password, contact Hansal to reset it for you.

---

## How to Add a New Student

1. Tap **Students** in the left menu (or bottom menu on mobile)
2. Tap the **+ Add Student** button (top right)
3. Fill in the student's information:
   - Name
   - Roll Number
   - Standard (class, e.g., "8th", "9th", "11th Commerce")
   - Gender
   - Date of Birth
   - Parent's name, phone, and email
   - Address
4. Tap **Add Student** to save
5. The student will appear in the list immediately

---

## How to Mark Attendance

1. Tap **Attendance** in the left menu
2. Select today's date and the standard (e.g., "8th")
3. Tap **Mark Attendance**
4. For each student, tap one of:
   - **P** — Present
   - **L** — Late
   - **A** — Absent
5. Tap **Save All** when you are done

---

## How to Enter Test Marks

1. Tap **Test Results** in the left menu
2. Tap the **Enter Marks** tab
3. Select the standard, test name, and subject
4. Type each student's marks in the box next to their name
5. Tap **Save Marks**

---

## How to Record a Fee Payment

1. Tap **Billing & Fees** in the left menu
2. Find the student in the list
3. Tap **Record Payment**
4. Enter the amount paid and payment method (Cash / UPI / Bank Transfer)
5. Tap **Record Payment** to save

---

## How to Send a Notification

1. Tap **Notifications** in the left menu
2. Tap **Send Notification** (top right)
3. Type a title for the notification (e.g., "Fee Reminder", "Holiday Notice")
4. Type your message
5. Choose the **Type** of notification:
   - General
   - Fee Reminder
   - Attendance
   - Resource
   - Exam
6. Choose **who should receive it**:
   - All Students
   - All Parents
   - All Admins
7. Optionally, select a specific **Standard** to send to (leave blank for all)
8. Tap **Send**

---

## How to Search for a Student

1. Tap **Students** in the left menu
2. Type the student's name or roll number in the **Search** box
3. The matching student(s) will appear in the list

---

## How Parents Can See Their Child's Progress

Parents can log in with their own email and password to see:

- **Attendance** — their child's attendance percentage and record
- **Test Scores** — latest test marks and grades
- **Fee Status** — pending balance and payment history
- **Notifications** — messages from the coaching class

1. Log in as the parent
2. The **Parent Dashboard** will show all this information automatically
3. Tap **View Billing** on the dashboard to see fee payment history

---

## How to Check the Dashboard

The home screen shows different information based on your role:

| Role | What you see |
|------|-------------|
| **Admin / Superadmin** | Total students, today's attendance, fees collected, pending fees, charts |
| **Parent** | Child's attendance, latest test score, pending fee balance |
| **Student** | Own attendance, own latest test score |
